making cheese

  • 2 slice of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheada
    • mozarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  1. spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. place bread on fryin pan
  3. add cheese on top of bread
  4. cover with second slice of bread
  5. turn over and fry for 2 mins
